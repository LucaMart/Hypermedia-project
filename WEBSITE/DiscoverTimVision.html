Where can i watch Tim Vision

Timvision is a service provided by Telecom Italia, for TIM customers and for customers of the other landline operators.

From any Tv through a special decoder, from compatible Smart TVs and Blu-ray(Samsung and LG), from the timvision.it site, from the TIMvision app for a PC connected with a TIM kay, from the app for Tablet and Smartphone, from the app for the Windows 8 operating stystem.
