TIM Vision

Description

With the TIMvision subscription you can choose between cartoons, movies, TV series, documentaries, music and the last seven days of the main RAI channels and La7. Anywhere, anytime.
More than 8,000 titles available, always on demand, to create your own schedule without commercial interruptions.

The other component of TIMVision is Videostore. Videostore is a rich video library from which you can rent or purchase the latest movies, series and cartoons.

The purchase or rental of the products included in the catalog is permitted from any fixed and mobile line.
You can pay online with credit cards Visa, Mastercard, American Express. Payment by mobile app on Tablet / Smartphone can be done with the remaining credit / TIM account or credit card.

Activation&Rules

How to subscribe

You can subscribe to the TIMVision Service:
- from this site, adding it to the basket
- from the mobile app
- or in a TIM shop.
In case you use the TIMVision mobile app: Download it from the App Store and enter the "Membership" section. You can choose as payment the remaining credit or your credit card.

Costs

Service costs: 1 € for the first month, then 5 € per month.
The contract length is indefinite. If a customer wishes to terminate the contract, she must access the TIMVision app from mobile or TV, and use the specific function.

From where can I use the service?

Any TV, using the specific TIMVision Decoder.
Smart TVs and Blu-rays (Samsung e LG). The site timvision.it. The TIMvision app for PC, Tablet e Smartphone.
note: A subscription to the service is valid for up to 6 devices.
